About Us

TBDesigns is an art and design company created by Travis and TaKia Beasley in 2017. Mr. and Mrs. Beasley each obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Studio Art degree from Albany State University. As an artistic duo, TBDesigns specializes in graphic design, photography, and 2D portraits. For photography, we are located in El Paso, Texas but we create drawings and graphics remotely.

We offer all of your graduation, engagement, and portrait photography needs! We can create your next logo or flyer! We create handmade charcoal or mixed media portraits.

Thank you for visiting our site! For inquiries, please visit our “Contact” section or email us at tbdesigns29@gmail.com
